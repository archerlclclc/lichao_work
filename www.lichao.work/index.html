My Resume

I am currently a 3rd year PhD student in the School of Information Sciences, University of Pittsburgh. Before that, I got my MSc degree from Imperial College London and BEng degree from both University of Edinburgh and Dalian University of Technology. I am now working with Prof. Palanisamy in UPitt. I am also a member of LERSAIS held by Prof. Joshi. My current research interests include database privacy, location privacy, social network privacy, differential privacy, distributed system security and IoT privacy.
